Request deletion of your kinboard data

This page is for users of the kinboard app (published by n1trospeed) who want their data deleted from our servers.

How to request deletion

  1. Email chongjin@wjohan.com with the subject "kinboard data deletion".
  2. Tell us the name of your board and roughly when it was created, so we can locate the right (encrypted) records.
  3. We confirm and delete within 30 days, and reply when it's done.

What gets deleted

  • All of your board's data on our sync server — the end-to-end encrypted note content (which we could never read) and its key-exchange messages.
  • If your board used Kin, the assistant's access and any suggestion material tied to your board.

What is kept, and for how long

  • Copies of your notes on your family's own devices are yours — we can't touch those. Deleting the app removes a device's local copy.
  • Routine server connection logs (IP addresses, timestamps) age out automatically within 30 days.
  • Nothing else is retained. We keep no accounts, profiles, or analytics.

Questions first? See the privacy policy or just write to the same address.
