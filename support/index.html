Support

How do I join my family's board?

Ask the person who started the board to tap the member dots and show you the invite QR — scan it from the join screen, or paste the code. The code is single-use and carries everything the app needs.

I lost my invite code

Codes are one-time. Just ask for a fresh one — it takes two taps.

Something else?

Write to chongjin@wjohan.com and a human who cannot read your board will get back to you.
